# METRICS — KPIs & Success Metrics

## North Star Metric

**Primary:** Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
**Secondary:** Audits Completed / Month

*Rationale: Audits = value delivered = paid conversion probability.*

---

## Product Metrics

### Acquisition
| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Waitlist Signups | Unique email signups | 500 by Month 3 |
| Trial Starts | New accounts created | 50/month by Month 3 |
| Trial→Paid Rate | Paid / Started Trial | > 20% |

### Activation
| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Time to First Audit | Minutes from signup to audit complete | < 5 min |
| Audit Completion Rate | Audits started / completed | > 80% |
| Aha Moment | User sees ≥1 saving recommendation | Day 1 |

### Retention
| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Monthly Churn | Customers lost / total | < 5% |
| Net Revenue Retention | MRR expansion - churn | > 100% |
| Weekly Active Users | Users who run an audit in 7 days | > 40% |

### Revenue
| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| MRR | Monthly Recurring Revenue | $20K by Month 12 |
| ARPU | MRR / customers | $80+ |
| LTV | ARPU × avg lifespan | $1,500+ |
| CAC | Total acquisition cost / new customers | < $150 |
| LTV:CAC | Ratio | > 5:1 |

## Business Health Dashboard

### Weekly Review
- New signups

- Audits completed
- Paid conversions
- MRR change
- Churn this week

### Monthly Review
- MRR growth rate
- Net Revenue Retention
- Top churn reasons
- Feature adoption rates
- CAC by channel

## Leading Indicators

If these are healthy, revenue follows:
1. **Audit completion rate > 80%** → Product is usable
2. **Time to first audit < 5 min** → Onboarding is working
3. **Aha moment on Day 1** → Users see value immediately
4. **NPS > 40** → Word of mouth will grow
