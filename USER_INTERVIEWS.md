# USER_INTERVIEWS — Research & Discovery

## Interview Framework

### Goal
Validate that the problem (uncontrolled AI spend) is real, painful, and worth paying to solve.

### Target Interviewees
- CTOs, VP Engineering, Technical Co-founders
- Companies with 10–200 employees
- Using 3+ AI tools

---

## Questions

### Problem Discovery
1. How many AI tools is your team currently using?
2. Do you have a single view of your total AI tool spend?
3. How did you decide which AI tools to adopt?
4. Have you ever audited your AI spend?
5. What's your current monthly budget for AI tools?

### Pain Probing
6. Have you ever canceled an AI subscription? Why?
7. How much time would you estimate reviewing AI tool value takes?
8. Have you ever had two team members paying for overlapping tools?
9. What's the biggest frustration with managing AI tools?

### Solution Validation
10. If you could see a single dashboard with all AI spend, how valuable would that be?
11. What would make you switch off a tool today?
12. Would you pay for automated spend optimization recommendations? How much?

## Patterns to Watch For

- [ ] Users don't know their exact AI spend
- [ ] Multiple people on same team subscribed independently
- [ ] CFOs want a report, not a dashboard
- [ ] CTOs worried about data privacy, not cost
- [ ] "We just cancel tools when they get expensive" (reactive, not proactive)
