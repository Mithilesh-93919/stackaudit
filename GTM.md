# GTM — Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target Market

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Primary:**
- Startup / Scale-up (10–200 employees)
- Engineering-led company
- $500–$5,000/month in AI tool spend
- CTO, VP Eng, or technical CFO as buyer
- Using 3+ AI tools with no centralized tracking

**Secondary:**
- Digital agencies with client AI budgets
- SMBs with growing AI adoption

---

## Positioning

**Tagline:** *Audit your AI stack. Cut waste. Keep what works.*

**One-liner:** StackAudit is the AI spend management tool for startups — find redundancies, optimize costs, and justify your AI budget in minutes.

**Category:** AI Spend Management (new category we're creating)

---

## Go-To-Market Phases

### Phase 1 — Founder-Led (Month 1-3)
- [ ] 10 free audits with target users (via LinkedIn, Slack communities)
- [ ] Weekly user interviews (see USER_INTERVIEWS.md)
- [ ] Build waitlist on landing page
- [ ] Goal: 100 waitlist signups, 5 paying pilots

### Phase 2 — Organic + Content (Month 3-6)
- [ ] SEO content: "AI tool cost comparison" keywords
- [ ] ProductHunt launch
- [ ] Twitter/X content: "AI stack audit" threads
- [ ] Goal: 50 paying customers, $5K MRR

### Phase 3 — Product-Led Growth (Month 6-12)
- [ ] Free audit tier (1 audit/month, 3 tools)
- [ ] Virality: shareable audit reports
- [ ] Integration with Stripe, QuickBooks for spend import
- [ ] Goal: 200 customers, $20K MRR
