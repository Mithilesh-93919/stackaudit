# ECONOMICS — Unit Economics & Business Model

## Revenue Model

StackAudit is a B2B SaaS with seat-based + feature-based pricing.

---

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
| Channel | Estimated CAC |
|---------|-------------|
| Content / SEO | $30 |
| ProductHunt / Organic | $50 |
| LinkedIn Outreach | $150 |
| Paid Ads (future) | $200 |
| **Blended Target** | **< $150** |

### Lifetime Value (LTV)
| Plan | ARPU | Avg Lifespan | LTV |
|------|------|-------------|-----|
| Starter | $29 | 18 mo | $522 |
| Pro | $79 | 24 mo | $1,896 |
| Enterprise | $400 | 36 mo | $14,400 |
| **Blended** | **~$80** | **~22 mo** | **~$1,760** |

### LTV:CAC Ratio Target
- Target: > 5:1
- Current estimate: ~12:1 (organic), ~8:1 (blended)

---

## Margins

| Cost Component | % of Revenue |
|---------------|-------------|
| AI API costs (OpenAI/Anthropic) | 8–12% |
| Infrastructure (Vercel + Supabase) | 3–5% |
| Support + ops | 5% |
| **Gross Margin Target** | **75–85%** |

---

## Pricing Tiers — Revenue Model

```
Starter ($29/mo)
  ├── Up to 10 tools
  ├── 1 audit/month
  └── Basic recommendations

Pro ($79/mo)
  ├── Unlimited tools
  ├── Unlimited audits
  ├── AI recommendations
  ├── Team member access
  └── CSV export

Enterprise ($299-500/mo)
  ├── All Pro features
  ├── Custom integrations
  ├── SSO/SAML
  ├── SLA
  └── Dedicated support
```
